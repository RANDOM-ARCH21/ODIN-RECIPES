Lasagna (Special Italian Dish!)

DESCRIPTION

The Italian favorite of lasagne or lasagna that we all know and love originated in Italy in the city of Naples during the Middle Ages. One of the first references to modern-day lasagne can be found in a 14th-century English cookbook that highlighted a dish with layers of pasta without the tomatoes.

INGREDIENTS

  • lasagne
  • ground meats
  • cheeses
  • tomato
  • vegetables
  • garlic
  • mozzarella

STEPS

  1. Mix ricotta and chesses(per recipe below) with an egg in a bowl, and set aside. No ricotta? No problem, use cottage cheese instead.

  2. Brown the onion, garlic, and meat in a pot on the stovetop. Add the pasta sauce and tomato paste & simmer a few minutes

  3. Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with noodles and bake untill bubbling
